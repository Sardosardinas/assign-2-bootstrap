About me

I love taking care of incredible teams! My background in communications sciences combined with my early experience in the LATAM Esports scene led me to develop a listen-first approach that I have applied as Office Coordinator at Riot Games in Mexico City, where our team’s commitment to excellence earned our office the #2 spot in 2017’s list of Great Place to Work. I am proud of what we’ve accomplished, and I want to continue testing myself with inspiring teams that challenge me to deliver awesome every day.


Right now I'm learning how to be a developer, because I want to be able to look at problems as a developer and offer another point of view for Operational teams to bring better tools to their services within the gaming industry
